Pasta

Description

Delicious italian dish

Ingredients

  • tomatoe paste
  • pasta (spaghetti, penne)
  • garlic
  • tuna
  • oil
  • basilic
  • cheese
  • spices (salt, black pepper)

Steps

  1. put oil, garlic, tuna, spices in a cooking pot and cook for a few minutes
  2. add tomatoes paste
  3. add water
  4. put pasta on boiling water for 7 minutes (al dente)
  5. add pasta to the sauce
  6. add cheese and basilic on top
  7. serve and taste!
