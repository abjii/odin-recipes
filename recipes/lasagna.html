Lasagna

Description

Delicious italian dish

Ingredients

  • tomatoe paste
  • lasagna noodles
  • onion
  • minced beef meat
  • cheese (cottage cheese, emmental, parmigiano)
  • basilic
  • spices (oregano, salt, pepper, garlic)
  • milk
  • flour
  • egg

Steps

  1. cook beef meat with garlic, onion, spices
  2. prepare white sauce : cook milk, flour, egg, salt, pepper
  3. prepare red sauce : cook onion, oil, precooked beef meat, pepper, water
  4. in a glass pan, put multiple layers of lasagna noodles, red sauce, white sauce, cheese
  5. cook in oven
  6. when cooking is finished, add parmigiano and basilic leaves on top
  7. serve and taste!
