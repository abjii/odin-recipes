Pasta

Description

Delicious indian dish

Ingredients

  • rice
  • meat
  • fresh tomatoes
  • garlic
  • oil
  • onion
  • pepper
  • spices (salt, pepper, anise, clove, cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, cumin)

Steps

  1. put oil, garlic, meat, spices, onion in a cooking pot and cook for a few minutes
  2. blend tomatoes and add the juice to the pot
  3. add water
  4. rinse rice and add it to the pot for a few minutes
  5. when the mix is boiling, lower the heat to the lowest intensity and add pepper
  6. after 20 minutes, the cooking should be finished
  7. serve and taste!
